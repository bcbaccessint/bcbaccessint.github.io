Who we are

We’re BCB US, backed by BCB, a transatlantic consumer and wholesale bank with global reach. We have deep roots. We may not have branches, but we’re part of a network of 85,000 people strong who move, lend, invest and protect money for customers and clients across 40 countries. In the U.S. market, we collaborate with over 25 top companies to deliver an array of consumer and small business credit card programs to fit any lifestyle.

Because we’re digital, your personal finances are always handy—from credit cards to savings, CDs, and loans. We offer simple tools that make it easy to plan, and there’s always a real person ready to help you when you need it.

We’re not just banking. We’re personal banking that helps you thrive. With great rates. Better rewards. Less work. And more time to focus on whatever you like. All with a personal touch.

We’re here to make banking work for you. Not the other way around.
